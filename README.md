# What Works, What Doesn’t in Startup Management Control

Public portfolio exploring what works (and what doesn’t) in startup finance and management control: budgeting, KPIs, cash runway and unit economics.

## Projects

1. **01_budgeting_and_scenarios**  
   Monthly P&L and cash-flow models for early-stage startups, with base/best/worst scenarios and key metrics (runway, break-even).

2. **02_kpi_system_what_works**  
   Practical KPI systems for SaaS / e-commerce, focusing on a few metrics that really matter.

3. **03_cash_burn_and_runway**  
   Analysis of cash burn patterns and runway in early-stage startups.

4. **04_cost_structure_and_unit_economics**  
   Cost structure, contribution margin and unit economics by business model.
